# FitMe: Project Context & Features

FitMe is a premium, AI-powered health and nutrition tracking application designed to simplify the complexity of maintaining a healthy lifestyle. It focuses on intuitive data entry, deep analytical insights, and gamified motivation.

## Core Features

### 1. Smart Nutrition Logging (AI-Powered)
- **Natural Language Input**: Users can describe their meals in plain text (e.g., "I had two eggs and a slice of avocado toast for breakfast") and the system parses the ingredients and nutritional data automatically.
- **Smart Logger Sheet**: A dedicated, floating interface for rapid AI-driven entry.

### 2. Comprehensive Macro Tracking
- **Real-time Dashboard**: Visual breakdown of calorie intake versus goals.
- **Macro Distribution**: Granular tracking of Protein, Carbohydrates, and Fats with detailed progress bars.
- **Detailed Nutritional Breakdown**: View micro-nutrients and full labels for every logged item.

### 3. Gamification & Consistency Engine
- **Momentum System**: A dynamic score that reflects the intensity and consistency of daily activity.
- **Activity Streaks**: Visual representation of consecutive days meeting goals, encouraging long-term habit formation.
- **FitMe Wrapped**: Periodic visual summaries of user achievements, milestones, and nutritional trends.

### 4. Flexible Weekly Budget (Rebalancer)
- **Dynamic Goal Adjustment**: Automatically recalculates daily calorie and macro targets based on previous days' performance within the same week.
- **Safety Clamps**: Ensures adjusted goals remain within a healthy ±20% range of the base target.
- **Monday Reset**: Goals automatically reset to the user's base targets at the start of every week.

### 5. Advanced Insights & AI Analysis
- **Diet Analysis**: AI-generated reports on eating patterns, identifying strengths and areas for improvement.
- **Smart Diet Planning**: Tools to set and adjust nutritional goals based on user progress and physical data.
- **Trend Visualization**: Interactive charts showing weight trends, calorie history, and macro balance over time.

### 6. Workout & Health Integration
- **Health Connect Sync**: Automatic ingestion of activity data (steps, workouts, active calories) from Android devices.
- **Activity Correlation**: Visualizing how workouts impact nutritional needs and goal completion.

### 7. Recipe & Food Management
- **Recipe Creation**: Build custom recipes with multiple ingredients for quick logging of recurring meals.
- **Favorites System**: One-tap access to frequently consumed foods and meals.
- **Custom Quantity Selection**: Flexible portion sizing with support for various units (grams, servings, etc.).

### 8. Robust Data Architecture
- **Flexible Authentication**: Secure login via Firebase or a fully functional "Guest Mode" for privacy-conscious users.
- **Cloud Sync & Backup**: Seamless data persistence across devices with manual and automatic backup options.
- **Responsive Theme System**: A premium, dark-mode-first aesthetic with fluid animations and haptic feedback.
